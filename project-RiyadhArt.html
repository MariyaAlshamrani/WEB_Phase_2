RIYADH ART — The City as a Canvas | فنون الرياض — المدينة كلوحة فنية

A movement that transforms Riyadh into a living gallery — where every street, park, and skyline tells a story through art. Riyadh Art celebrates imagination and culture, connecting people and places with creativity that inspires, uplifts, and transforms.
